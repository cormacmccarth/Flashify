Welcome to Flashify!


Here you can create your own flashcards in order to help you to learn any materials that you are currently studying.


To begin, simply click the New Card button.


Once you have created a flashcard you just need to click on it to reveal the answer to the displayed question.


Once you have finished with your cards, just click the Delete Cards button.


You can also toggle between light and dark mode by clicking the Mode button.
